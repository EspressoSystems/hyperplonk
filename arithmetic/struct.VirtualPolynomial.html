Expand description

A virtual polynomial is a sum of products of multilinear polynomials; where the multilinear polynomials are stored via their multilinear extensions: (coefficient, DenseMultilinearExtension)

  • Number of products n = polynomial.products.len(),
  • Number of multiplicands of ith product m_i = polynomial.products[i].1.len(),
  • Coefficient of ith product c_i = polynomial.products[i].0

The resulting polynomial is

$$ \sum_{i=0}^{n} c_i \cdot \prod_{j=0}^{m_i} P_{ij} $$

Example: f = c0 * f0 * f1 * f2 + c1 * f3 * f4 where f0 … f4 are multilinear polynomials

  • flattened_ml_extensions stores the multilinear extension representation of f0, f1, f2, f3 and f4
  • products is [ (c0, [0, 1, 2]), (c1, [3, 4]) ]
  • raw_pointers_lookup_table maps fi to i
