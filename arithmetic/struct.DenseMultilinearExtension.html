pub fn relabel_inplace(&mut self, a: usize, b: usize, k: usize)

Relabel the point inplace by switching k scalars from position a to position b, and from position b to position a in vector.

This function turns P(x_1,...,x_a,...,x_{a+k - 1},...,x_b,...,x_{b+k - 1},...,x_n) to P(x_1,...,x_b,...,x_{b+k - 1},...,x_a,...,x_{a+k - 1},...,x_n)
